0376 - Greater Angle-Wing Katydid


Microcentrum rhombifolium
Male
58mm (including wingtip)

USA: FLA: Seminole Co.
Cross Seminole Trail, Mile Marker 0.75
28°36'53.10"N 81°16'22.8"W, 16m
On Bush near Street Light
Nighttime, Still Air

12 October 2021
Z. Gill

Notes: Watched it fly from a treetop to another high branch by the street light, threw some sticks and watched it fly and land on a lower bush within reach; Flew silently; While in the Insect Kill Jar, it looks like it expelled a glob of whiteish meat/guts out of its ovipositor
